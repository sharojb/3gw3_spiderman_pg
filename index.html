The Evolution and History of Spiderman

The Different Lives of Spider-Man

Spider-Man has been brought to life multiple times, with each version of the movies having their own plot, cast of characters, and special Spider-Man suit. The famous actors that brought Peter Parker to life are: Tobey Maguire in the Sam Raimi films, Andrew Garfield in the Marc Webb films, and Tom Holland in the Marvel Cinematic Universe films.

The Current Spider-Man

The most recent movies feature Tom Holland as Peter Parker. This version of Spider-Man has three subsequent movies: 2017’s Homecoming, 2019’s Far From Home, and 2021’s No Way Home.
